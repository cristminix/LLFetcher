      Here's the plan

      “

      - Has someone asked you to make a training video? Wait what, have this told you to make one? Oh that's cold. And I'm guessing you're not an expert video maker either. Yet, don't worry, I think I can help, follow me. (casual uplifting music) Hi, I'm Rob Glass, I'm a video journalist and that means I get involved in all aspects of video production. Planning, the filming, the editing and right there, those three, that's how we're going to tackle this course. We're going to be doing some pre-production planning, that way we can make sure we say exactly what we need to and no more. Maybe just lose that bottom bit, better. And then we'll take a look at production. We won't take a deep dive into every camera you could use, we've got other courses for that. But we will take a look at how to keep your shots steady and level. Finally, we'll sew up all our loose ends in post-production, so our training videos always look jaw-droppingly good. Maybe just a close-up there, superb. So whether you're on your own or part of a team, the important thing is to create engaging video for your learners. But most of all have some fun because when you're having fun your audience is having fun, isn't that right?! (cheering) Yes. (casual uplifting music)
